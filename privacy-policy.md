# Good Morning Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2026

Good Morning ("Good Morning," "we," "us," or "our") is a mobile app designed to help users start their day with a morning routine, wake-up reminders, app blocking, caffeine timing, and simple wellness insights.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects or accesses, how it is used, where it is stored, and when it may be shared.

Developer: Christopher Ambrose  
Contact:  goodmorningroutineapp@gmail.com

## Information We Collect or Access

### Account Information

Good Morning requires an account to use the app. You may create an account using email and password or sign in with Google.

We may collect or receive:

- Your email address
- Your Firebase user ID
- Your Google account sign-in information, if you choose Google Sign-In
- Your display name, if provided by your sign-in provider

Authentication is provided through Firebase Authentication and Google Sign-In.

### Profile and Routine Information

Good Morning stores information you provide or create in the app, including:

- Your name
- Your wake-up time
- Your selected alarm days
- Whether onboarding is complete
- Your current and longest streak
- Your selected routine template
- Custom routine activities, names, order, duration, and timer settings
- Tomorrow's planned wake time and routine
- Caffeine timer settings, including whether the timer is enabled and the selected delay

This information is stored on your device and is also synced to our Firebase Firestore backend so your profile can be restored when you sign in.

### Morning Session and Usage Information

Good Morning creates session history when you complete or miss a morning routine. This may include:

- Date of the session
- Routine name
- Morning score
- Completed activity count
- Required activity count
- Optional activity count
- Routine duration
- Activity names
- Planned wake time
- Routine start time
- Minutes after planned wake time when the routine started
- Whether app blocking was active
- Whether blocked apps stayed blocked until the routine was complete
- Whether blocked apps were unlocked early
- Whether the morning was missed
- A short accountability note generated by the app

Morning session history is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData storage and synced to Firebase Firestore under your user account so your history can be restored on a new device.

### Mood Check-In

After a routine, Good Morning may ask how your day is starting. If you choose to answer, the app stores your selected mood, such as "Great," "Good," "Okay," "Low," or "Rough."

Mood check-ins are stored locally on your device as part of morning session history and synced to Firebase Firestore with that session history.

### App Blocking and Screen Time Information

Good Morning uses Apple's Screen Time, Family Controls, and Managed Settings frameworks to let you choose distracting apps, app categories, or web domains to block during your morning routine.

Good Morning stores the Screen Time selection tokens needed to reapply your choices. Apple does not expose the names of selected apps to the app in a way that Good Morning displays or stores as readable app names. The app stores a count and summary such as "apps & categories."

Based on the current app code:

- Your selected app/category/domain tokens are stored on your device.
- The app's blocking-enabled setting and unlock condition may sync to Firebase Firestore.
- The selected app/category/domain tokens themselves are not included in the Firestore profile sync.

Good Morning uses this permission only to block selected apps, categories, or web domains until your routine is complete or until the configured unlock condition is met.

### Notifications and Alarms

Good Morning may request permission to send notifications and schedule alarms. The app uses these permissions to:

- Send wake-up reminders
- Schedule notification-based wake alarms
- Schedule system alarms through Apple's AlarmKit where available
- Provide snooze and "Start Morning" actions

Alarm times, alarm days, and related scheduling information are used to provide these features.

### Live Activities

Good Morning uses Apple's ActivityKit and Live Activities to show routine progress and caffeine timer status on the Lock Screen or Dynamic Island where supported.

Live Activity information may include:

- Current routine activity
- Remaining time
- Next activity
- Progress
- Caffeine timer availability time
- Caffeine delay length

The app does not use Live Activity push notifications in the current implementation.

### Calendar Access

If you use the morning brief feature, Good Morning may request access to your calendar to show your first upcoming calendar event for the day. The app displays the event time and title in the morning brief.

Based on the current app code, calendar event information is used for display in the app and is not stored in your Good Morning profile or synced to Firebase.

### Location and Weather

Good Morning may request When-In-Use location access to show current weather in your morning brief. The app uses your device location to request current weather from Apple's WeatherKit service.

Based on the current app code, weather information is displayed in the app and is not stored in your Good Morning profile or synced to Firebase.

### Apple Health

Good Morning can connect to Apple Health to read sleep analysis data so the app can show a sleep summary and help you understand how rest may relate to your mornings. The app also requests permission to read mindful sessions and share mindful sessions.

Based on the current app code, Good Morning reads sleep data to produce a short sleep summary. The summary may be displayed in Settings or Stats. The app does not store Apple Health sleep samples in its own database.

Apple Health data is handled under Apple's HealthKit rules. You can manage Health permissions in the Apple Health app or iOS Settings.

### Subscription Status

The current app code includes fields for premium status and premium expiration date. If subscriptions are enabled, Good Morning may store whether premium access is unlocked and when that access expires.

Payment processing details depend on the final subscription implementation and must be completed before publication. In-app purchases and subscriptions on iOS are handled by Apple. If RevenueCat is added, RevenueCat's role and data handling must be added to this policy before publishing.

## How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

- Create and manage your account
- Save and restore your profile
- Build and run your morning routine
- Schedule wake alarms and reminders
- Block selected apps during your morning routine
- Track routine completion, streaks, and morning scores
- Show insights about your routine, mood, blocking, wake consistency, and sleep summary
- Display weather and calendar information in your morning brief
- Show Live Activity progress
- Manage subscription access, if subscriptions are enabled
- Maintain and improve app functionality

## Where Information Is Stored

Good Morning stores some information locally on your device and some information in Firebase Firestore.

Stored on your device:

- SwiftData app data
- Morning session history
- Mood check-ins
- Screen Time selection tokens
- AlarmKit alarm ID
- Notification handoff state
- Some caffeine timer state

Synced to Firebase Firestore:

- Profile settings
- Wake time
- Routine settings
- Custom routine data
- Tomorrow plan data
- Streaks
- Blocking-enabled setting and unlock condition
- Caffeine timer enabled setting and delay
- Premium status and expiration date, if used
- Account email
- Morning session history
- Mood check-ins attached to morning session history

Handled by third-party or Apple services:

- Firebase Authentication account data
- Google Sign-In authentication data
- Apple notifications and alarms
- Apple Screen Time app-blocking controls
- Apple Health data
- Apple WeatherKit weather requests
- Apple Calendar access
- Apple in-app purchase processing, if subscriptions are enabled

## How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We share information only as needed to operate the app, including:

- With Firebase, for authentication and synced profile and session storage
- With Google, if you choose Google Sign-In
- With Apple services, when you use Apple permissions such as Screen Time, notifications, alarms, Health, Calendar, WeatherKit, Live Activities, or in-app purchases
- With RevenueCat, if RevenueCat is added for subscription management before launch

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or security of users, the app, or others.

## Your Choices

You can manage many permissions through iOS Settings, including:

- Notifications
- Alarms, where available
- Screen Time / Family Controls
- Location
- Calendar
- Apple Health
- Live Activities

You can also sign out from the app.

The current app code does not include an in-app account deletion or data export feature. To request deletion or export of account-related data, contact us at goodmorningroutineapp@gmail.com. Account deletion and data export procedures must be finalized before this policy is published.

## Data Retention

Good Morning keeps your account information, routine settings, synced session history, and related app data for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide the app.

If you delete your account in the app, Good Morning deletes your Firebase account, your Firestore profile and synced session data, and local app data for the signed-in account on that device. Some information may remain temporarily in backups, logs, or records where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or legitimate business purposes.
